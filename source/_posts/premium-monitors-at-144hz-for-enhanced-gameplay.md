## Premium Monitors at 144Hz for Enhanced Gameplay

 If you want to ascend into ultrawide immersion, there are plenty of gaming monitors available, ranging from affordable 60Hz screens to high-end 240Hz behemoths. While most of these options will provide excellent immersion and the ability to see more of your game at once, we think 144Hz is that sweet spot where ultrawide monitors offer the best balance of performance and affordability.

 Whether it's your first time going ultrawide or you're a veteran looking to upgrade, 144Hz ultrawide monitors hit the sweet spot for most gamers. They tend to cost less and still deliver the speed and responsiveness needed to get the perfect headshot in competitive shooters or give you an advantage in fast-paced games.

## Choosing the Best 144Hz Ultrawide Gaming Monitor

 While there's no shortage of 144Hz ultrawide gaming monitors, finding one that fits your needs can be an uphill task. If you don't want to go through all the options, the LG UltraGear 34GP83A-B is an excellent pick for most people. It strikes the sweet spot for both gamers and professionals, and you can even overclock it to 160Hz for increased speed and responsiveness.

 If you have more to spend, you can't go wrong with Dell's Alienware AW3821DW. It has better color accuracy, brilliant HDR picture quality, solid cross-platform VRR support, and offers more screen real estate. For gamers on a tight budget, the GIGABYTE G34WQCA provides the perfect compromise between gaming performance, picture quality, and price.

 Ultimately, the best 144Hz ultrawide monitor will depend on your budget and the types of games you plan to play. For competitive shooters and racing titles, combining the 144Hz refresh with quick response time performance and low input lag will give you the best performance. On the other hand, if you play more open-world exploration games, color accuracy, HDR, and higher contrasts should be at the top of your priority list.
