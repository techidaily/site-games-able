For 30 years, Optus and Cisco have been providing a vast array of telecommunications and IT solutions for Australian organisations and consumers. Enzo Cocotti - Senior Director, Fixed Networking Products, Optus Enterprise and Business; Rodney Hamill - Managing Director, Partner, Routes to Market and SMB Sales, Australia and New Zealand at Cisco; and Melvin Arulanthu - Director of Partnerships, Optus Enterprise and Business; describe how this unique partnership has disrupted the marketplace and built value for their customers, both now and into the future.

To find out more about how you can build your future with Optus Enterprise visit [FutureNow – Optus Enterprise and Business](https://www.optus.com.au/enterprise/futurenow).
