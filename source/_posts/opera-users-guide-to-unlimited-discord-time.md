## Opera Users' Guide to Unlimited Discord Time

### Key Takeaways

* Opera GX is offering a one-month free trial of Discord Nitro to its users until June 2024.
* To get the free trial, Opera GX users need to download the browser, visit Opera GX's Discord Nitro promotional page, and link their Discord account.
* Keep in mind that there are terms and conditions, such as having no active Nitro subscription in the past 12 months and canceling the trial before the 30-day mark to avoid being charged $9.99/month.

 Opera GX, the gaming-focused web browser, has launched an exciting collaboration with Discord, offering users a one-month free trial of Discord Nitro.

 The promotion is currently available until June 2024, and there are some terms and conditions to consider, but it's well worth checking out to get a month of free Discord Nitro.

## How to Get Free Discord Nitro With Opera GX

 Getting a month's worth of Discord Nitro through Opera GX is pretty simple, and all you need is to follow these steps:

1. Launch [Opera GX](https://www.opera.com/gx) .  
   * **Download:** Opera GX for [Windows & macOS](https://www.opera.com/gx) |[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/opera-gx/id1559740799) |[Android](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/7251228/type/dlg/sid/UUmuoUeUpU2031412/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opera.gx) (All free)
2. Visit Opera GX's [Discord Nitro promotional page](http://www.opera.com/gx/discord-nitro) .
3. Press the**CLAIM NOW!** button to be redirected to the Discord website.  
![Discord Nitro Claim Now Opera GX](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/discord-nitro-claim-now.jpg)
4. Link your Discord account to Opera GX to apply for the promotion.  
![Log Into Discord](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/log-into-discord.jpg)

 Keep in mind that this promotion is only available to Opera GX users. You may not visit the promo page from any other web browser. For instance, we tried accessing the promo page through Microsoft Edge, but the**CLAIM NOW!** button is grayed out and inaccessible:

## Terms and Conditions for Getting Discord Nitro for Free

 This special promotion is like a free Nitro trial; you must sign in using a payment option to register.

1. Choose the form of payment you want to proceed with.  
![Subscribe Free Discord Nitro](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/subscribe-free-discord-nitro.jpg)
2. Once you've filled in the data, press**Get Nitro Monthly** .  
![Free Discord Nitro Finalize Transation](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/free-discord-nitro-finalize-transation.jpg)
3. Enjoy using Discord Nitro.  
![Discord Nitro Activated](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/discord-nitro-activated.jpg)

 However, there are a few things that you need to know about the free Discord Nitro promotion before you access it:

* This promotion is only valid for users without an active Nitro subscription within the past 12 months.
* You'll need to present a valid form of payment at sign-up. For this reason, a small sum of money ($0.99) will be withheld and returned to you. This is for verification purposes.
* Once your 30-day trial is over, your Nitro subscription will transform into a monthly recurring Nitro subscription.
* To prevent Discord from charging you the $9.99/month fee, you should cancel your free trial before the 30-day mark.

This offer is available until July 16th, 2024, 11:59 PM ET.

## Benefits of Discord Nitro Over Regular Discord

 Discord's free version offers more than enough for the average user. However,[Nitro brings plenty of perks](http://www.makeuseof.com/is-discord-nitro-worth-it/) that make the Discord experience much more enjoyable.

 For starters, you can use custom emojis, stickers, and soundboards across compatible platforms and servers. Nitro also allows you to share your screen at high resolutions (1080p 60FPS), which is great for streamers, and you also gain access to enhanced profile personalization tools, such as custom Discord banners.

 Other handy Discord Nitro perks include more server access (up to 200 servers), expanded messaging limits (up to 4,000 characters), and more super reactions to use in chat. Note that [Discord Nitro differs from Discord Nitro Classic](https://www.makeuseof.com/discord-nitro-vs-discord-nitro-classic/) , with Discord Nitro receiving more perks.

 This collaboration between Opera GX and Discord is a great opportunity for gamers to grab a free month of Discord Nitro and experience everything its premium tier has to offer.
