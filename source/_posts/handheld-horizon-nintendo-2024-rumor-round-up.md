## Handheld Horizon: Nintendo 2024 Rumor Round-Up

### Key Takeaways

* Nintendo Switch 2 is expected to release in 2024, but its official name is still unknown. It is likely to support backward compatibility.
* The podcast explores Nintendo's impact on the handheld gaming market and speculates on its future direction.

 The Nintendo Switch 2 is coming, possibly in 2024\. But at this time we don't know what it's called, only that it will probably be backward compatible.
